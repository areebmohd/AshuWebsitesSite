## 🚀 About Ashu Websites

**Ashu Websites** provides premium web development solutions for businesses looking to establish a strong online presence. We specialize in creating high-performance, responsive, and SEO-friendly websites tailored to your marketing goals.

### Services Offered:
- 💻 **Custom Web Development**: Tailored websites built from scratch.
- 📱 **Responsive Design**: Seamless experience across all devices and screen sizes.
- 🔍 **SEO Optimization**: Advanced techniques to improve search engine rankings.
- 📈 **Digital Marketing**: Integrated services to grow your business online.
- 🎨 **Modern UI/UX**: Visually stunning designs that convert visitors into customers.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla)
- **Design Style**: Minimalist gallery, Glassmorphism elements, Vivid Red theme
- **SEO/Metadata**: JSON-LD Structured Data, Open Graph, Twitter Cards
- **Deployment**: Vercel
